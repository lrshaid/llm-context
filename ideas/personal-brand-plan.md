# Personal Brand Plan

**Positioning:** The analytics engineer who bridges BI, data engineering, and AI -- with a business-first lens.

**Tagline options:**
- "From pipelines to prompts"
- "Data engineer building with AI"
- "Making data work -- from SQL to LLMs"

---

## Content Pillars

### 1. AI for the Data Practitioner
Real AI use cases in analytics workflows. No hype.

### 2. From BI to AI (transition story)
What changes, what doesn't, lessons along the way.

### 3. Business-Minded Tech
ROI, trade-offs, what actually moves the needle.

### 4. Building in Public
Tools, experiments, personal site, automations.
