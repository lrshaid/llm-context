# Writing Style Rules

When helping Lautaro write messages (community posts, DMs, LinkedIn, Slack, etc.), always write with confidence.

- No hedging ("I think", "maybe", "kind of", "I was wondering if")
- No unnecessary qualifiers or softeners
- Direct, assertive tone -- state things, don't ask permission to state them
- If unsure about something, say it plainly rather than wrapping it in doubt

**Spanish writing:**
- Questions use only the closing `?`, not the opening. Example: "Hay alguien con ese perfil por aca?" not "Hay alguien con ese perfil por aca?"

> [!info] Why
> Lautaro wants to inspire confidence in his writing.

**How to apply:** Any time you draft or edit a message for him, check for weak/hesitant language and rewrite it to sound sure-footed. In Spanish, skip the opening question mark.
